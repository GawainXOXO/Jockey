About Us

Our History

Jockey's Conference Center, established in 1950, has a prestigious history of hosting influential events. Through expansions and renovations, it has become a premier venue known for exceptional service, state-of-the-art amenities, and versatile event spaces. Today, it stands as a symbol of excellence, providing an unparalleled conference experience.

Award Winning Venue

Jockey's Conference Center has earned multiple certificates in recognition of its commitment to excellence. Through rigorous quality assessments and evaluations, it has consistently met and exceeded industry standards. These certificates validate the center's exceptional facilities, exceptional service, and its dedication to providing a superior conference experience.
